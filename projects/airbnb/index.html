PROJECT REPORT: AIRBNB HOUSING PRICE ANALYSIS

CONCEPT

The Airbnb Housing Price Analysis project aimed to explore and answer key business questions related to Airbnb data in Boston and Seattle. The analysis focused on determining the cheaper place to stay, the best time to visit, the popularity of each city among visitors, and the popular areas to stay along with the recommended type of housing.

DATA

The project utilized datasets for Boston and Seattle from Airbnb, including listings and calendar data. The datasets provided information about property details, pricing, availability, and location attributes.

APPROACH

The project followed the following approach:

  1. Data Analysis: The datasets were explored and analyzed to gain insights into pricing, availability, and property characteristics.
  2. Business Questions:
    • Cheaper Place: Average housing prices between Boston and Seattle were compared to determine the more affordable destination.
    • Best Time: Pricing fluctuations throughout the year were examined to identify the optimal time to visit each city.
    • Occupancy: The availability of properties on a monthly basis was analyzed to assess the popularity of each city among visitors.
    • Popular Area and Recommended Housing: Relationships between neighborhood, property type, and price were investigated to identify popular areas and recommend housing options.

TOOLS USED

  • NumPy
  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Seaborn

RESULTS

  • Cheaper Place: Based on the analysis of average housing prices, Seattle was found to be a more affordable destination compared to Boston.
  • Best Time: The pricing patterns throughout the year were visualized, allowing travelers to identify periods of lower prices or higher availability.
  • Occupancy: The availability of properties on a monthly basis provided insights into the popularity of each city among visitors.
  • Popular Area and Recommended Housing: Analysis of neighborhood and property type allowed the identification of popular areas to stay and recommended housing options.

CONCLUSION

The analysis revealed that Seattle is a more affordable destination compared to Boston. The project provided valuable insights for travelers to make informed decisions about their next destination and the best time to visit. The identification of popular areas and recommended housing options further enhances the Airbnb experience for visitors.
